Grandma Zellie's

Chocolate Bundt Cake

Serving size: 8-10
Prep time: 10 minutes
Bake time: 50 minutes
Cool time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

Duncan Hines Yellow Cake Mix

4 eggs

1 cup of milk

1/2 cup of oil

1 cup chocolate chips

Preparation

1) Combine all the ingredients–except the chocolate chips and powdered sugar–in a mixer bowl. Beat 1-2 minutes, add box of instant chocolate pudding. Beat for 5 minutes. Stir in the chocolate chips.

2) Pour into a well-greased and floured bundt pan. Bake at 350 degrees for an hour, or until a toothpick comes out clean.

3) Cool the cake for 15 minutes and then remove it from the pan. Cool completely before dusting with powdered sugar.

4) Enjoy!
